About the App

A smarter way to guide phone habits.

What it solves

EduLock helps reduce passive app opening by placing short educational challenges between the user and selected apps.

Who it is for

Parents, students and focused users who want screen time to include learning prompts instead of only restrictions.

Why it is different

It reframes app locking as a productive nudge, combining app control with education in a clean Android experience.

Features

Productive controls for everyday devices.
