About the App

A calm calendar for fasting and festival reminders.

What it solves

Important vrat and festival dates can be easy to miss. Vrat Reminder brings calendar visibility and timely reminders into one app.

Who it is for

People who observe Hindu fasting days and festivals and want dependable reminders without manual calendar maintenance.

Why it is different

The product is focused on clarity, offline calendar access and respectful reminder design rather than a crowded generic calendar.
