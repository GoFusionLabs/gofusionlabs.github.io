About the App

Focused Hindi handwriting practice for modern learners.

What it solves

Hindi handwriting takes repetition and feedback. Hindi Dictation gives learners a structured place to write, check and improve without waiting for manual review.

Who it is for

Students, parents and self-learners who want a calm Android practice tool for Hindi letters, words and dictation-style exercises.

Why it is different

AI recognition turns handwriting practice into an interactive loop, combining clear exercises with immediate feedback and a polished mobile interface.

Features

Guided practice with intelligent feedback.
